About This Cause

Two boys need a lifesaving bone marrow transplant.

A few years ago I was on one of my many humanitarian trips. This time to Uganda. I was traveling to many remote villages teaching about Feminine periods, Cycle beads and “Days for Girls” pads. A young reporter, Lutale Obeid, who works for Uganda’s Bukedde TV station was traveling with me and documented my trip to show a report on tv. As we traveled I learned of his 2 sons who both suffer from Sickle cell disease. I learned of the many times he has had to spend all his savings on buying blood to give the boys a blood transfusion. They are getting worse!!! Their visits to the hospital is becoming more and more frequent. They need a full bone marrow transplant in order to survive. The doctors have ok’ed the surgery, we just need the funds. $40.000 per child. A crazy amount we cannot reach alone. If you at all can help this cause it is greatly appreciated. We are all trying to do what we can but need your help please.
