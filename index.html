The Cause

Leith (6) and Bisaso (10) are two young children who are battling a serious medical condition called sickle cell anemia. This condition causes their red blood cells to become misshapen and unable to carry enough oxygen throughout their bodies. As a result, they are constantly tired and in pain, and they are at risk of life-threatening complications. They have been surviving on blood donations and transfusions but they have stopped working. The boys last hope is a lifesaving bone marrow transplant. However, at a price of $40,000 per child their father Obeid cannot afford the cost of the transplant on their own, and they desperately need your help. By donating to this GoFundMe campaign, you can give Leith and Bisaso the chance to receive the lifesaving treatment they need and give them the opportunity to live healthy, happy lives. Please, help these two brave children by giving what you can today. ANY AMOUNT HELPS!
