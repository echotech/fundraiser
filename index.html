Leith and Bisaso

Leith is 6 and Bisaso is 10

Their Condition

The boys were born with sickle cell anemia. You can read more about the disease here.

The Treatment

Transfusions have stopped working and the boys both need bone marrow transplants. Bone marrow transplantation (BMT) is currently the only known cure for sickle cell disease. It involves replacing the abnormal stem cells residing in bone marrow with healthy cells from an eligible donor.

Care & Cost

Their doctors have approved them for bone marrow transplants. But the cost is $40,000 per child and their parents can not afford it.

Their Story

Leith (6) and Bisaso (10) are two young children who are battling a serious medical condition called sickle cell anemia. This condition causes their red blood cells to become misshapen and unable to carry enough oxygen throughout their bodies. As a result, they are constantly tired and in pain, and they are at risk of life-threatening complications. They have been surviving on blood donations and transfusions but they have stopped working. The boys last hope is a lifesaving bone marrow transplant. However, at a price of $40,000 per child their father Obeid cannot afford the cost of the transplant on their own, and they desperately need your help. By donating to Venmo or this GoFundMe campaign, you can give Leith and Bisaso the chance to receive the lifesaving treatment they need and give them the opportunity to live healthy, happy lives. My name is Obeid Lutale (37yrs), I’m a Ugandan by birth. I work at New Vision Printing and Publishing Company Uganda Ltd as a journalist. I have had a very hard time for the last 10 years, managing day to day life and looking after my two sons who are sick. I am the only earning member of my family. My wife is currently looking after our newborn 5 month old baby. I am struggling financially to ensure that Leith and Bisaso get the best treatment, education, and welfare since their life is extremely expensive. Leith and Bisaso need $10,250 a year to cover their medications and treatments. They need to have have a healthy diet and hydrate always to maintain their immune system. To keep warm we need to have plenty of jackets, socks, and gloves. We have a place to call home, which I’m paying rent for as well as security and bills every month at $400. With the influenza outbreak in schools, this has made the situation even worse. Until now I haven't been able to clear the debt. Soon after Bisaso fell sick too and we were back to hospital for outpatient treatment which was costly as well. I am writing this to request your financial assistance. Donations of any kind can be of use for my children.

I pray that God allows you to do so in any capacity.

Kind regards
